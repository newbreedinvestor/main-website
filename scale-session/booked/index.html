Your Call Agenda

What will happen on your Scaling Session

On this 30–45 minute call, we will:

  1. 1 Review your current situation – market(s), deal flow, and marketing efforts.
  2. 2 Map out how a Niche Data + Outbound + AI system would plug into your business.
  3. 3 Run the simple “one good deal pays for it” math based on your real numbers.
  4. 4 If it’s a fit, walk through how our program works and what it looks like to work together. If it’s not, we’ll tell you straight and point you in the right direction.

This is not a free coaching call. It’s a working session to decide if we should build this system with you now or not.

Show Up Ready

How to prepare (so we can hit the ground running)

Before we talk, please:

  • 📊 Know your basic numbers: deals in the last 12 months, average profit per deal, and rough monthly marketing spend.
  • 🏭 Have 1–3 recent or potential deals in mind so we can use real examples.
  • 🔒 Make sure you can be in a quiet place, not driving or distracted.
  • 👪 If you make financial decisions with a spouse or partner, try to book a time they can join.

If something comes up and you can’t make it, please use the reschedule/cancel link in your confirmation email at least 24 hours in advance. We only take a limited number of these calls each week and no‑shows take a spot from someone else.
